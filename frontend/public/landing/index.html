About FederCare

FederCare is a privacy-preserving AI health network that lets hospitals, doctors and patients share intelligence — never raw data. We connect care across an entire region while keeping every record on the device it belongs to.

How does federated learning protect patient data?

Raw patient records never leave the hospital. Each participating site trains a local model and shares only encrypted weight updates with the FedAvg aggregator — the global model improves while the data stays put.

How accurate is the X-Ray pneumonia detector?

Our MobileNetV2-based classifier achieves a 99.5% recall on the held-out validation set across 5,856 chest radiographs. It is designed as a triage aid, never as a replacement for a radiologist's report.

How does the Emergency SOS workflow work?

A patient triggers SOS from the mobile app, their GPS location is broadcast to the nearest available ambulance, and the driver receives turn-by-turn navigation along with patient context. The receiving hospital is alerted in parallel.

Can my hospital join the federated network?

Yes. After verification by the Super Admin, hospitals are issued a Flower FL client that participates in scheduled training rounds. No raw data, no PHI, no external storage — just gradient updates.

How are prescriptions verified before delivery?

Pharmacists verify the digital prescription against the doctor's signed record before dispatch. The patient confirms receipt with a one-time password, closing the loop with auditable delivery.

Is FederCare a commercial product?

FederCare is an academic MCA Final Year Project (2025–2026) at Mar Thoma Institute of Information Technology, Ayur, Kollam. It is intended as a reference implementation of a privacy-preserving healthcare network.
